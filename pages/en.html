Arrival Information

If you are arriving by air, take the Airport Coach to Korsvägen (first stop in Göteborg). From Korsvägen, either walk 700m, or take one of the following trams from platform E to Scandinavium (next stop):

  • 2, direction Marklandsgatan
  • 6, direction Kortedala
  • 8, direction Angered
  • 13, direction Wieselgrensplatsen

If you are arriving by train, either walk 1.5 km, or take one of the following trams from Drottningtorget, platform A (2) or B (13) to Scandinavium (second stop):

  • 2, direction Mölndal
  • 13, direction Sahlgrenska
